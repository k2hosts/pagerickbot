RickBot

Advanced Discord Security & Moderation

RickBot is a highly configurable Discord security bot built with JavaScript and Node.js. Every protection is tunable to fit your server — from thresholds and punishments to whitelisted roles and logging channels.

💣 Anti-Nuke
Detects mass deletions of channels, roles, and bans. Automatically punishes and reverses damage in real time.
🚨 Anti-Raid
Monitors join rates and triggers automatic lockdown when a raid is detected, keeping your community safe.
⚡ Auto-Mod
Filters spam, mass mentions, invite links, and toxic content with fully configurable rules and actions.
📋 Audit Logging
Logs every moderation action, security event, and server change to a dedicated channel of your choice.
🎫 Ticket System
A built-in support ticket system so your staff can handle reports and member issues without extra bots.
⚙️ Highly Configurable
Every feature is customisable — set thresholds, punishments, whitelists, and log channels your way.
